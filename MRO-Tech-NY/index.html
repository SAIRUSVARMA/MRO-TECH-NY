Mission Statement

MRO Tech NY is dedicated to delivering exceptional consulting services for the built environment, empowering building owners and managers to optimize their assets, reduce operational costs, reduce emissions, support ESG initiatives and enhance the occupant experience. We achieve this through our commitment to technical excellence and unparalleled customer service.

Core Values

  • Technical Excellence: We strive for precision, innovation, and best practices in all our services.
  • Customer Focus: We prioritize our clients' needs, listening attentively and responding promptly.
  • Collaboration: We foster open communication, mutual respect, and teamwork among our employees, clients, and partners.
  • Integrity: We operate with transparency, honesty, and ethics in all our interactions.
  • Innovation: We encourage creativity, experimentation, and continuous learning to stay ahead of industry trends.

Vision Statement

Our vision is to be the trusted partner of choice for building owners and managers in the United States, recognized for our expertise, reliability, and passion for delivering tailored solutions that drive efficiency, sustainability, and value.
